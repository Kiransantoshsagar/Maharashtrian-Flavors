# Maharashtrian-Flavors

Maharashtrian Flavours Online Platform

A full-stack web application designed to promote and deliver authentic Maharashtrian cuisine through a modern digital platform. The system connects customers with local home chefs, restaurants, and food vendors, allowing users to explore traditional dishes, place online orders, make secure payments, and experience Maharashtra's rich culinary heritage from anywhere.

🚀 Key Features

🍽️ Authentic Maharashtrian Menu: Browse a wide variety of traditional Maharashtrian dishes with detailed descriptions and pricing.

👨‍🍳 Home Chef & Vendor Registration: Enables local home chefs and food vendors to register and showcase their specialties online.

🛒 Online Food Ordering: Customers can easily add dishes to the cart and place food orders through a simple interface.

💳 Secure Payment System: Supports secure online payments with multiple payment methods.

📧 Email Notifications: Sends order confirmation and status updates to customers via email.

📦 Order Management: Customers can view order details while administrators manage incoming orders efficiently.

⭐ Customer Reviews & Ratings: Users can provide ratings and feedback to improve service quality.

📱 Responsive User Interface: Fully responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

📊 Admin Dashboard: Manage customers, food items, vendors, orders, and platform activities from a centralized dashboard.
